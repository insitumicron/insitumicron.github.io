Miniature Mechanical Testing

Our state-of-the-art testing solutions allow for precise characterization of materials at the microscale. Testing in-service components is essential to understand failure — it helps design new materials, manufacturing methods, and parts.

Miniaturised testing allows site-specific testing, which is vital to comprehend material behaviour under thermal and mechanical stresses during manufacturing. Additive manufacturing demands this as well.

In-Situ Characterization

Conduct real-time testing under realistic conditions to gather critical data on material performance. In-situ characterization augments the information obtained because it's carried out under actual loading conditions.

The vital information collected is essential for identifying failure mechanisms and allows material manufacturers to design better materials. It's especially powerful for measuring single crystal elastic constants — which are challenging and expensive to determine otherwise.

XRD Raman Spectroscopy Optical Microscopy SEM

Failure Analysis

Our comprehensive failure analysis services help identify the root causes of material or product failure, ensuring improved designs and increased reliability.

Whether it's a fractured component from an industrial process or a research specimen behaving unexpectedly, we help you understand why — and what to do about it.
