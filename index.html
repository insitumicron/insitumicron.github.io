Miniatrised Mechanical Testing

Testing in-service coomponents is essential to understand their failure. This helps in designing new materials, manufacturing methods, and the components itself. Miniaturised testing allows site-specific testign, that is key to understand material under thermal and mechanical strain of conventional manufacturing. Additive manufacturing also demands site specific tesing because of its inherit nature.

Insitu testing

Insitu characterisation augument the information obtained as it is carried out under real-time loading. The vital information collected is essential for identifying the failure mechanism and allowing material manufacturers to design new materials. It is being employed for measuring single crystal elastic constants for new materials that are challenging and expensive to determine otherwise.
