Our Journey

Great things happen when you plan them well. Here's how InsituMicron went from a research idea to a company building the next generation of material testing instruments.

Milestones & Roadmap

From inception to the future — every step of the InsituMicron story

2019

The Idea

Research at IIT Madras identified a critical gap: no affordable miniature mechanical testing instrument existed that could integrate with common characterization tools.

✓ Completed
2020

First Prototype

Built and tested the first working prototype of the miniature mechanical testing fixture, validating the core concept and design principles.

✓ Completed
2021

IIT Madras Research Park Incubation

InsituMicron was formally incubated at IIT Madras Research Park, gaining access to world-class infrastructure, mentorship, and the broader IIT Madras research ecosystem.

✓ Completed
2022

Product Validation & First Customers

Validated the instrument with academic research partners. First successful in-situ XRD experiments conducted using our miniature fixture — proving the core value proposition.

✓ Completed
2023

Multi-Mode Capability

Expanded the instrument to support tension, compression, and bending modes in a single compact fixture — a key technical breakthrough that differentiates us in the market.

✓ Completed
2024

Custom Software Suite

Launched our proprietary software for experiment control, real-time data acquisition, and analysis — completing the end-to-end testing workflow for customers.

✓ Completed
2025

Scale & Expand

Scaling manufacturing capabilities, expanding the customer base across academia and industry, and launching partnerships with key characterization equipment manufacturers.

▶ In Progress
2026 & Beyond

Global Reach

Targeting international research institutes and industrial R&D labs. Building the next generation of instruments with automated testing, AI-assisted analysis, and broader compatibility.
