Who We Are

InsituMicron is a deep-tech startup incubated at IIT Madras Research Park, Chennai. We design and build miniaturized mechanical testing instruments that integrate seamlessly with characterization equipment like electron microscopes, X-ray diffractometers, and Raman spectrometers.

Our goal is simple: give researchers the tools to observe what actually happens inside a material as it deforms — in real time, at the microscale.

What Drives Us

Material failure causes everything from aircraft accidents to infrastructure collapse. Understanding failure at the microscale is the only way to truly prevent it. Traditional testing misses the picture — it measures after the fact, at the macro level.

We believe researchers deserve better. That's why we build instruments that let them watch materials fail, deform, and recover — with full characterization running simultaneously.
